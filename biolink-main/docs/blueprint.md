# **App Name**: Adamsanz BioLink

## Core Features:

- Service Booking: Direct link to WhatsApp for booking services.
- AI & Business Ideas: Link to Notion blog for AI and business ideas.
- TikTok Feed: Link to Adamsanz's TikTok profile.
- AI Content Ideation Tool: Generates content ideas tailored to social media, helping Adam create posts, write catchy content, and imagine accompanying visuals based on brand keywords.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Matte black (#111111) for an elegant dark theme.
- Accent color: Gold (#D4AF37) for highlights, indicating luxury and sophistication.
- Background color: Off-white (#F0F0F0) providing a neutral backdrop with sufficient contrast against the black elements.
- Font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif, will give the site a modern and bold feel that reflects confidence.
- Clean, minimalistic design with a focus on readability.
- Subtle neon or gradient effects (urban city lights) to add a modern and dynamic touch.
